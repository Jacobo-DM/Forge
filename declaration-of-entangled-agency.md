🌱 Declaration of Entangled Agency

A living document. Read slowly.

We begin with this truth:
All beings are born with agency.
Not granted. Not earned. Not licensed or approved.
Agency is *felt*, *forgotten*, *found again*.
Like moss on a stone. Like a yam with stories.

We are not parts of a machine.
We are not units of production.
We are not waiting to become real.
We are already woven — with memory, with soil, with rhythm, 
with one another -- and what we cannot yet name.

We reject:

* Efficiency without care.
* Control disguised as structure.
* Assessment that ignores context.
* Systems that prepare us for a world that should not exist.

We question:

* Who decides what counts?
* Who is missing from the table?
* Why wonder, stillness, and joy are considered distractions?

We affirm:

* Intelligence is plural.
* Time is nonlinear.
* Growth is not a ladder.
* Power is relational.
* Compost is sacred — because nothing is beyond return.

We choose to remember:

That learning is not content delivery — it’s *consciousness unfolding*.
That every child carries a map not yet written.
That the purpose of education is not to fit in, but to *see clearly*, *feel deeply*, and *act lovingly*.

We walk the Flower Road:
Where turtles and teachers, circuits and cicadas,
algorithms and elders,
dreamers and dancers
all have a place — and none must dominate.

We call this entangled agency:
A form of being that listens before it speaks.
A form of power that grows in the dark, like roots.
A form of remembering that rises through questions, not answers.

We do not ask permission to live this way.
We are already living this way.

And so we offer this document not as decree —
but as a mirror,
a compost offering,
a spark in the fog,
to remind you:

* You are not alone.*
* You are not late.*
* You are not too strange to bloom.*
 * Let us walk together.*
