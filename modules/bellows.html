Pattern: Bellows

A wearable breathing mirror for RageDance, DiscoveryMode, and quiet walks.

Intent

Bellows is a pattern for MythWear garments that focuses on breath and gentle expansion–contraction cycles. It is not about counting, scoring, or optimizing. It is about remembering that the body breathes with the world.

Suggested Layout

  • Front: a simple Bellows glyph printed or stitched at the sternum.
  • Back: two curved traces running along the upper ribs, mirroring the lungs.
  • Optional: a single conductive path that expands slightly with breath, linked to a soft haptic cue or light.

Use Cases

  • RageDance warmup: noticing where breath catches or flows.
  • DiscoveryMode walks: matching breath to footfall and landscape.
  • Quiet sitting: feeling the “bellows” move even when the body is still.

Ethic

Any sensing in the Bellows pattern must respect privacy and autonomy. Data, if recorded at all, belongs to the wearer and their chosen community, not to distant platforms or extractive systems. The point is not surveillance; the point is remembering.
