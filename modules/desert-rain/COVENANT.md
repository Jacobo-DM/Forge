# Desert Rain Covenant

Desert Rain is released as part of the Forge Commons.

You may fork, adapt, build upon, and transform this work.

But one principle must remain intact.

## Preserve the Pause

Desert Rain exists to protect a form of reading that requires stillness and attention.

Forks may change the interface, the visual language, or the implementation.

Forks may explore new sensing methods, new interaction models, or new environments.

However:

The system must not be optimized for speed, engagement metrics, or addictive interaction.

Desert Rain is not a product.
It is a practice.

Any derivative work should preserve the core intention:

To create a space where readers slow down long enough to actually encounter the text.

If a fork removes the pause entirely, it is no longer Desert Rain.

It is something else.

That is allowed.

But it should carry another name.
