Desert Rain

Move near the text, scroll, touch, or arrive by keyboard. Words gather, brighten, and fall into reading. Adjust cadence, clustering, and reading shape.

The desert does not speak all at once. It gathers at the edge of attention, then falls through reading like rain. Words arrive through nearness, cadence, and care.

Movement begins the reading field. Nearness clarifies local words. Reading shape and spacing can be adjusted for comfort and accessibility.
