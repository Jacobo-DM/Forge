# The Braid Remembers in Contact

*A quiet root note.*

Not a banner.  
Not a brand statement.  
A tuning fork hidden in the wall.

## Core lines

None of them glowing alone.

The work must remain encounter-shaped.

The braid remembers why it sings only in contact.

We remember what we are for in meeting.

## Cosmology of relation

A river that never meets a stone forgets how to sing.  
A stone that meets a river remembers how to sing.  
A song that meets river and stone remembers why it sings.

## Quiet interpretive note

River may be read as flow, feeling, time, movement, change.  
Stone may be read as form, witness, resistance, memory, boundary.  
Song is the living thing born between them — not pure motion, not pure stillness, but resonance.

## Teaching note

Contact restores memory.  
Relation restores song.

Method may return through contact.  
Meaning returns through relation.

## Placement logic

This note should remain findable from anywhere in the ecosystem, but quiet.

It is not meant to lead as a slogan or appear as a polished front-facing statement.  
It is here to help the work remember what it is for when polish, abstraction, performance, or aesthetic capture begin to pull it away from encounter.

This note belongs especially close to DiscoveryMode, but may quietly govern the whole garden.

## Reminder

The braid does not remember through polish alone.  
It remembers in contact.
